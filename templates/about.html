About TxT Jokes

We wanted to make you laugh, and we wanted to do it differently.

TxTJokes is a Twilio sms example application created during the Twilio and Dev.to hackathon 2020.

Created by Glenn Paquette, Txt jokes is a Django web application which uses PyJokes, & Twilio to send you nerd jokes by sms.
The site is styled with Bootstrap 4 and Bootswatch and is redered with Django's built in templating engine.

Further development includes schedualed joke delivery, sending gifs, expanding the joke library, and sending via alternative means - such as slack or whatsapp.
We further would like to collect stats such as # of jokes sent, and accept feedback from recepients.
